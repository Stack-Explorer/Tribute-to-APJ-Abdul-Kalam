A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a renowned aerospace scientist, visionary leader, and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India's missile development programs, including the successful creation of the Agni and Prithvi missile systems, which significantly strengthened the country's defense capabilities. Throughout his career, he contributed extensively to ISRO and DRDO, leading several groundbreaking projects in space research and nuclear technology.
